Komodo Dragons' Realm

Behold the living legends of Komodo National Park, the majestic Komodo dragons, as they roam freely in their natural habitat. These ancient reptiles are not only remarkable symbols of Indonesia's biodiversity but also hold cultural significance in local folklore and traditions. Their prehistoric presence serves as a reminder of the need for conservation and the delicate balance between human life and the natural world.
