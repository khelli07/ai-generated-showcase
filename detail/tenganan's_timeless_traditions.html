Tenganan's Timeless Traditions

Step into the ancient village of Tenganan, where time seems to stand still amidst its well-preserved traditions and customs. The village's distinct architecture, featuring intricately woven bamboo walls, showcases the expertise of the local craftsmen. Here, the double-ikat weaving technique produces renowned Gringsing textiles, reflecting Tenganan's rich cultural identity. Visiting this secluded hamlet offers a glimpse into a world where age-old customs, rituals, and craftsmanship thrive in harmony.
